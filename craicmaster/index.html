WELCOME

Where’s the craic aims to provide users the easiest and fun way to search for businesses (food & beverage and music & cultural venues) based on location and search criteria delimited by them.

On the other hand provides a tool for business on the field to show their place, create a profile and real­time information about the place to attract customers, build loyalty and promote their business

We use location intelligence (geolocation) to build meaningful experiences for our users. Discover new places near where you are, enjoy the city and all that it has to offer! From concerts to networking events. You will always find something suitable to your preferences

Business can find new audiences and promote your business on our application. Build a loyal attendance group. We make it easy to delight your attendees to keep them coming back and interested in your business with exciting offers.
